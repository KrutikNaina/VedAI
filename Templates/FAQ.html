What is VedAI

VedAI is an AI-powered spiritual chatbot that provides guidance based on Hindu scriptures like the Bhagavad Gita, the four Vedas (Rig, Sama, Yajur, Atharva), and other sacred texts. It helps users with life problems, spiritual insights, daily Choghadiya, and Katha planning.

Can VedAI help with personal problems?

Yes! You can ask about stress, karma, success, relationships, or any life issue, and VedAI will provide relevant shlokas, meanings, and practical solutions based on Hindu wisdom.

Is VedAI free to use?

Yes! VedAI offers free access to essential features, including Bhagavad Gita shlokas, life guidance, Choghadiya, and basic spiritual insights.

Can I use VedAI on my mobile phone?

Yes! VedAI is fully mobile-friendly and works on all smartphones and tablets through a web browser.

Can I save my conversations with VedAI?

No, currently, VedAI does not store or save user conversations. Each session is private, and once you exit, the chat history is not retained.
